Our Processes

Our Swiss Turning Solutions

We master a wide range of precision machining processes, enabling us to create complex technical components with accuracy and superior quality.

Turning

High-precision turning for cylindrical and conical components.

Milling

Material removal using rotating tools to create complex shapes and precise finishes.

Drilling

Creation of precise holes in various materials with different diameters and depths.

Boring

Finishing and boring of existing holes to achieve tight tolerances.

Threading

Creation of internal and external threads with high dimensional accuracy.

Tapping

Specialized process for creating threads and complex helical shapes.

Knurling

Creation of textures and patterns on surfaces for improved grip or aesthetics.

Stamping

Shaping and forming components through controlled pressure.

Gear Hobbing

Advanced process for manufacturing high-precision gears and toothed components.
